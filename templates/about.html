ℹ️ About PawConnect AI

Our mission, vision, and commitment to animal welfare through technology.

Project Overview

Technology in Service of Compassion

PawConnect AI is a sustainability-focused platform that harnesses technology to protect India's stray dog population by connecting citizens, NGOs, and volunteers in real time.

Every day, millions of stray dogs struggle with hunger, injury, and illness. PawConnect AI transforms the way communities respond — making it easy for anyone to report, volunteer, and get AI-guided help instantly.

✅ Rapid complaint reporting with unique Complaint IDs
✅ Real-time status tracking (Submitted → In Progress → Rescued → Closed)
✅ AI-powered guidance via PawBot chatbot
✅ Volunteer and NGO coordination
✅ Analytics dashboard & interactive complaint map

Problem Statement

The Challenge We're Solving

😔
No Reporting Channel

Citizens who spot injured or sick stray dogs have no easy way to report and get help. Phone calls to authorities are rarely answered.

🕐
Delayed Response

Without a coordination system, even reported cases take days to receive attention — causing preventable suffering and deaths.

🔌
Disconnected Volunteers

Thousands of willing volunteers and NGOs have no centralized platform to coordinate effectively with citizens reporting animals in need.

India has an estimated 62 million stray dogs — the highest in the world. Without coordinated intervention, millions suffer needlessly each year.

Why PawConnect

The Story Behind the Platform

PawConnect AI was created out of a genuine desire to bridge the gap between compassionate citizens and the animals that desperately need their help. Existing animal welfare systems rely on fragmented phone calls, word-of-mouth, and manual coordination — leading to slow response times and animals suffering unnecessarily.

By building a digital-first, data-driven platform, PawConnect AI empowers every citizen to become part of the solution — whether by reporting a stray animal in distress, volunteering their time, or simply spreading awareness. The platform brings transparency, speed, and scale to animal welfare that was previously impossible without technology.

Proposed Solution

How PawConnect AI Solves It

01
Instant Reporting Platform

Citizens submit complaints via a simple web form with photo upload, getting a unique Complaint ID (PC001) for tracking.

02
Volunteer Network

Registered volunteers (food providers, rescue teams, medical volunteers) are matched with complaints by location.

03
AI Chatbot Guidance

PawBot provides 24/7 guidance on feeding, first aid, contacts, and reporting — reducing barriers to action.

04
Analytics & Transparency

Admin dashboard with charts, maps, and status tracking ensures accountability and data-driven decision making.

Project Objectives

Our Goals

  • Enable any citizen to report a stray dog emergency in under 2 minutes
  • Achieve a target 2-hour response time for all submitted complaints
  • Build a network of 100+ volunteers across 10+ Indian cities
  • Partner with 15+ NGOs for medical support and adoption services
  • Rescue and rehabilitate 500+ animals per year
  • Educate communities on humane treatment and ABC programmes
  • Provide a transparent, data-driven model scalable to other cities and species
