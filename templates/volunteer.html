Food Provider

Set up regular feeding stations, provide nutritious food, and maintain fresh water supplies for stray dogs in your area.

Responsibilities:
  • Feed dogs at consistent times daily
  • Maintain clean feeding spots
  • Report new dogs in your area
  • Coordinate with other providers
🚑
Rescue Volunteer

Respond to emergency reports, safely transport injured or distressed dogs to shelters or veterinary care.

Responsibilities:
  • Respond to SOS reports promptly
  • Handle dogs safely and humanely
  • Coordinate with vets and shelters
  • Update case status in dashboard
💉
Medical Assistance

Provide veterinary care, vaccinations, wound treatment, and support ABC (Animal Birth Control) programs.

Responsibilities:
  • Assess and treat sick/injured dogs
  • Support vaccination drives
  • Assist with ABC programs
  • Advise other volunteers on dog health
