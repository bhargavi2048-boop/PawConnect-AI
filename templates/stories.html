📍 Chennai, Tamil Nadu
Bruno's Road to Recovery

Bruno was found injured near Anna Salai with a broken leg after being hit by a vehicle. A PawConnect user submitted a report at 9 AM. By noon, our rescue volunteer team had reached him, and he was transferred to Blue Cross veterinary care. After 3 weeks of treatment, Bruno made a full recovery and was adopted by a loving family.

📍 Bengaluru, Karnataka
Bella's Mange Treatment

Bella was reported through PawConnect with severe mange — a painful skin condition that had left her nearly bald. A medical volunteer from our network administered weekly treatments over 6 weeks. Today, Bella's coat has fully grown back, and she lives with the very volunteer who treated her.

💉 Medical Treatment 🏠 Foster Care ⭐ Full Recovery
Week 1 — Identified & reported by a student
Week 2 — Treatment started by volunteer
Week 8 — Full coat regrowth
Week 10 — Adopted by her caregiver ❤️

📍 Avadi, Tamil Nadu
The Community Feeding Station

After repeated "hungry dog" reports in the Avadi locality, our platform connected 5 food providers. They organized a daily feeding station serving 15 stray dogs. This initiative now runs autonomously as a community effort, with PawConnect serving as the coordination hub.

🍖 Community Feeding 🤝 5 Volunteers 🐾 15 Dogs Fed Daily
Month 1 — 8 reports of hungry dogs flagged
Month 1 — Volunteers connected via platform
Month 2 — Daily feeding station established
Ongoing — 15 dogs receive meals daily 🍖

📍 Hyderabad, Telangana
Max's ABC Programme Success

Max was a territorial male dog causing conflict in a residential colony. Instead of removal, our PawBot chatbot guided the reporting citizen to understand ABC (Animal Birth Control) programs. PawConnect connected them with a local NGO, Max was sterilized, vaccinated, and returned — becoming the colony's beloved guardian.

💉 ABC Programme 🏘️ Community Harmony 🤖 AI-Guided
Day 1 — Conflict reported via PawConnect
Day 2 — PawBot explained ABC options
Week 2 — NGO partner performed ABC
Week 3 — Max returned as colony protector 🛡️
